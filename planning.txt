User Stories:
1. As a user, I should be able to flip a card
2. As a user, I should be able to win/lose the game
3. As a card, I should be able to flip over
4. As a memory game, I should have sets of matching cards
5. As a user, I should be able to match sets of cards


Bronze:
- Display back of cards
- Display front of cards
- Flip cards

Silver:
- Track pairs of cards when they are flipped

Gold:
- Add images to cards
- Have different decks
